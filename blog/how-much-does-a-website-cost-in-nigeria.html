How Much Does a Website Cost in Nigeria? 10 Questions Every Business Owner Should Ask Before Hiring a Web Designer

Published: June 29, 2026 | By Obara Digital

If you're a business owner in Nigeria searching for a web designer, you're probably asking the same questions thousands of other entrepreneurs ask every day.

How much should a website cost? How long will it take? Will it appear on Google? Can you trust the person building it?

These are important questions because choosing the wrong web designer can cost you money, waste your time, and leave your business with a website that doesn't generate customers.

1. How Much Does a Website Cost in Nigeria?

Website pricing depends on the type of website, required features, and the experience of the web designer.

Website Type Typical Price Range Features Included
Portfolio Website ₦150,000 – ₦350,000 Basic pages, contact form, mobile responsive
Business Website ₦300,000 – ₦800,000 CMS, blog, SEO setup
E-commerce Website ₦700,000 – ₦3,000,000+ Products, cart, payment integration
Custom Web Application ₦2,000,000+ Custom functionality and dashboards

What Affects Website Pricing?

  • Number of pages
  • Custom design requirements
  • E-commerce functionality
  • SEO optimization
  • Content creation
  • Third-party integrations
  • Maintenance requirements

The cheapest quote isn't always the best. A professionally built website should be viewed as an investment, not an expense.

2. How Long Does It Take to Build a Website?

  • Portfolio website: 5 days – 1 week
  • Business website: 1 – 2 weeks
  • E-commerce website: 3 – 6 weeks
  • Custom web application: 1 – 3 months

3. Will My Website Appear on Google?

A website does not automatically rank on Google simply because it exists.

  • Proper SEO setup
  • Fast loading speed
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • High-quality content
  • Technical SEO
  • Google Search Console integration

4. Can I Edit My Website Myself After It's Completed?

Yes. Most modern websites can be built in a way that allows you to update content yourself.

Will I be able to update my website myself after launch?

5. Why Is My Current Website Not Bringing Customers?

  • Poor SEO
  • Slow loading speed
  • No call-to-action
  • Poor mobile experience
  • Weak content
  • No marketing strategy

6. Do I Really Need SEO?

Absolutely. SEO helps customers find your business online when they search Google.

7. Should I Choose WordPress or a Custom Website?

WordPress

  • Easier to manage
  • Flexible
  • Excellent for blogs

Custom Websites

  • Faster performance
  • Greater flexibility
  • Unique functionality

8. What Ongoing Costs Should I Expect?

  • Domain renewal
  • Web hosting
  • Website maintenance
  • SEO services
  • Technical support

9. How Do I Choose the Right Web Designer in Nigeria?

  • Check their portfolio
  • Ask about SEO
  • Evaluate communication
  • Ask about support
  • Request transparent pricing

10. What Should Be Included in a Professional Website Package?

  • Domain setup
  • Hosting setup
  • SSL certificate
  • SEO setup
  • Google Analytics
  • Google Search Console
  • Training and support

Final Thoughts

Hiring the right web designer can significantly impact the growth of your business. Focus on value, expertise, communication, and long-term support.
